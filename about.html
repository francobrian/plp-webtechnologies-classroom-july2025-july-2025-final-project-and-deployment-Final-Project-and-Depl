About Kilifi Sands Resort

Discover our story and commitment to exceptional hospitality

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Kilifi Sands Resort has been a beacon of luxury and relaxation on the Kenyan coast. Nestled along the pristine beaches of Kilifi, our resort was created with a vision to provide guests with an unparalleled experience that blends modern comfort with the natural beauty of the Indian Ocean.

Our dedicated team is passionate about creating memorable stays, whether you're here for a romantic getaway, a family vacation, or a solo adventure. We strive to offer personalized service and a warm, welcoming atmosphere that makes every guest feel at home.

Our Mission

At Kilifi Sands Resort, our mission is to provide exceptional hospitality while preserving the natural beauty of our surroundings. We are committed to sustainable practices, supporting local communities, and delivering unforgettable experiences for our guests.

Why Choose Us?

  • Prime beachfront location with stunning ocean views
  • Luxurious accommodations with modern amenities
  • Exceptional dining with fresh, local ingredients
  • A wide range of activities, from water sports to cultural tours
  • Committed to eco-friendly and sustainable practices
